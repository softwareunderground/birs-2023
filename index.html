About

We are happy to announce a 2 day workshop in which the communities of Fatiando a Terra and SimPEG will join together to set the roadmap for enhancing open-source tools for geophysics.

The workshop will take place at the Banff International Research Station (BIRS) from July 28 to 30, 2023, surrounded by astonishing mountain landscape.

Information

  • Where?
